Alerts & Recommendations

Increase Soil Moisture

Soil moisture levels are critically low (28%). Your tomato plants require 40-60% for optimal growth. Recommend immediate irrigation for 15 minutes.

Reduce Humidity

Greenhouse humidity (68%) is approaching levels that may promote fungal growth. Consider increasing ventilation for 30 minutes to reduce to 55-60%.

CO₂ Enrichment Opportunity

Current CO₂ levels (720ppm) are good, but could be optimized. Increasing to 800-1000ppm during peak daylight hours may improve photosynthesis by 15-25%.
